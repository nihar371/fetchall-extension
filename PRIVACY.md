# Privacy Policy for FetchAll

**Last Updated:** 7th June, 2026

FetchAll ("the Extension") is designed to respect your privacy and keep your data secure. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your information.

**1. Data Collection and Usage**
FetchAll operates entirely locally within your Google Chrome browser. The Extension reads the content of your active Gmail tab solely for the purpose of identifying, extracting, and downloading email attachments at your direct request. 

**2. No Data Transfer**
FetchAll **does not** collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, email content, or attachments with the developer, third-party servers, or any external services. All processing, including the creation of the final ZIP file, happens locally on your machine.

**3. Permissions**
* **activeTab & Host Permissions (`mail.google.com`):** Used strictly to allow the extension to run on Gmail and extract attachments when you click the extension icon.
* **downloads:** Used solely to save the compiled ZIP file to your local computer.
* **storage:** Used to temporarily store the local state of the automation sequence.

**4. Third-Party Sharing**
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or email data to outside parties.

**5. Contact**
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
